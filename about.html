About

We are the organizing committee for Saint George Pride 2017. We are a collection of skilled professionals and passionate advocates who are donating our time and resources for an all-out pride festival in Saint George next year and building a foundation to keep this festival going for years to come.

Saint George pride 2017 will take place in June of 2017 and we are in full swing planning mode. We are looking for volunteers to help run the festival, vendors to sell food & other products, and sponsors to found the festival. The festival will take place in Vernon Worthen Park and will include a pre-party, inter-denominational faith service, family time, pride ceremony, and light-updance party. We plan to paint the city rainbow for one weekend and show people that we are an integral and valued part of this community.

This is a non-profit festival to raise donations. Any money raised will go directly towards securing activities and events for the pride festival. We also plan to present a scholarship to a student in the LGBTQIA community, and donate excess funds towards the future inclusion center in Saint George.

As we have already begun the planning stages we are urgently seeking donations to cover costs for venue rental, activities, and other costs involved with hosting this event. We are seeking donations immediately so that we can make our plans concrete. For more information about donating, visit our donation page or talk to Cody Ham, our festival chairperson at 818-720-2156 or codymham@gmail.com.

The St George/Southern Utah community desperately needs exposure to the LGBTQIA+ community. We believe that coming out in full color, and showing the Saint George community that we are not to be feared is of utmost importance. We also believe that in helping to fund the future inclusion center in Saint George will create a legacy of love and understanding, which will provide community cohesion for years to come.
