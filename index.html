How Pride Benefits The Community

The St George Pride Festival is a non-profit event focused on strengthening our community, raising awareness, promoting acceptance and raising money to benefit LGBTQ youth in our community. Last year we had over 1,000 people in attendance and raised over ,000 for the new LGBTQ Resource Center at Dixie State University. All of the money raised went directly to funding scholarships, a reading library and bringing advocates to campus.
