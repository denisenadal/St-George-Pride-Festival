Ready to Help?

I'm

And I want to

The St. George Pride Festival is entirely non-profit, with all of our proceeds going directing into the LGBTQ+ community, so we depend on our donors and sponsors to make this event happen. Last year we were able to raise over $2,000 for scholarships and funding for the DSU LGBTQ+ Resource Center. We look forward to being able to raise even more money next year.

In order to be able to donate our proceeds, we need help covering the costs of the event. Some of our costs include booking the park and renting A/V equipment. You can contact Cody Ham, chair of the St. George Pride committee, at 818-720-2156 or codymham@gmail.com about how to become a sponsor.

As a sponsor you will receive the sincere gratitude of our community, as well as being listed on our program and website as an event sponsor. Sponsoring Pride means supporting the most visible LGBT event in St. George.

Volunteer

Volunteering is a great way to get involved in your community and develop leadership skills. You can also meet new people and make friends. Volunteering for Pride also looks greats on resumes. It’s family-friendly, and people of all ages can help.

There’s a variety of ways to volunteer for Pride. Here are some of our staffing needs for the event:

  • Attendant at the donation/information booth
  • Security at the event
  • Running A/V equipment
  • Vendor Liaison
  • Set up
  • Clean up

Fill out our form to get started

Vendors

Being a vendor at St. George Pride gives you a great business opportunity. Over a thousand people attended the first Pride in 2016, and we’re anticipating even more people this year! Our attendees will need food, drinks and want to purchase LGBT-gear while at the event. Bringing your business to the St. George Pride is not only a great way to make money, but also to show your values to the community and develop a lasting relationship with local customers.

Use out form to sign up as a vendor and reserve your spot at the event
