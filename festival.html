How to Find Us

Driving Directions

From the North: SLC, Cedar City, etc

Take I-15 South to exit 8, St George Blvd. Head west on St George Blvd. Turn left on to 400 East. The park will be on your right after 3 blocks, at 300 South & 400 East.

From the South East: Colorado City, Kanab, etc

Head West on Hwy 389/59. In Hurricane, turn right on 100 South, In one block, turn left on Hwy 9/State Street. When Hwy 9 merges with I-15, head south on I-15. Take exit 8, St George Blvd. Head west on St George Blvd. Turn left onto 400 East. The park will be on your right after 3 blocks, at 300 South & 400 East.

From the South West: Mesquite, Las Vegas, etc

Take I-15 North to exit 6, Bluff Street. Head North on Bluff Street and turn right on 700 South. After passing Dixie High School, turn left onto 400 South. Vernon Worthen Park will be on your left after 4 blocks.

Public Transit

Vernon Worthen Park is accessible by public transit. Take the St George SUNTRAN #2 Riverside Route to the southeast corner of Vernon Worthen Park at 300 South & 400 East.

Parking Information

There is free street parking at the StGeorge Pride Festival. Park on any of the streets around Vernon Worthen Park, but be considerate of our neighbors.There is NO PARKING at the Recreation Center across from the park (Parking lot is for Recreation Center visitors only).
