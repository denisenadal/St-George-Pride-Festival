Resources

DSU LGBTQ+ Resource Center

The Dixie State University LGBTQ+ Resource Center supports DSU’s LGBTQ+ students, staff, and faculty, as well as educates the campus’s straight and cis community on how to be fully inclusive and welcoming of LGBTQ+ identities. The Resource Center has informational booklets, a lending library for students, and supportive and caring sta. The Resource Center also hosts discussions, lectures and events for the public, and support groups for students. While the DSU LGBTQ+ Resource Center is focused on helping LGBTQ+ students, no member of the community is turned away.

Mentors

Want to speak to a member of the local LGBTQ community? Do you have questions about your orientation or gender identity that you’d like a mentor to answer? Do you need advice on how to be an ally to a friend or family member? The mentors at Dixie State University’s new LGBTQ+ Resource Center are happy to help. While the DSU LGBTQ+ Resource Center is focused on helping LGBTQ+ students, no member of the community is turned away. Visit DSU’s LGBTQ+ Resource Center office in person or use their online contact form.

LGBT-Friendly Care Providers

Visit DSU’s LGBTQ+ Resource Center or email lgbtcenter@dixie.edu to obtain a list of LGBT-friendly care providers, such as doctors and therapists. A great resource for finding LGBT-friendly care providers is found at the GLMA webpage, which has a provider database. There are no providers listed in St. George, but they can be found in nearby Las Vegas.

Education

Looking to educate yourself on LGBTQ+ issues and campaigns? Need more information on your rights, resources in the community, or how to find a support network of peers? Need information on how to be a better ally and support a loved one? Of perhaps need statistics and information about the current status and history of the LGBTQ+ community for a project or report? Visit any of the following links, which are a list of some the most thorough resources on the community, its history, and its current struggles.
